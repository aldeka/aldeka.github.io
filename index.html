About Us

Cortland "Blake" Setlow first met Karen Rustad in October 2009 in Philadelphia, PA when he sat across the table from her at their mutual friend Asheesh's birthday party. Blake was visiting Asheesh and other Philly friends; Karen was working at Asheesh's startup-at-the-time, OpenHatch. After a raucous birthday dinner, Blake convinced Karen to walk back across Philadelphia with him rather than wait for the bus and lent her his coat. He thought she was cute. She thought he was interesting. Neither said so to the other person.

Two years later, Blake and Karen re-met at the MIT Mystery Hunt in January 2012. Their puzzle hunt team, the Manic Sages, won the Mystery Hunt that year. Blake and Karen kissed a whole lot and walked around snowy, quiet Cambridge together.

Blake and Karen started dating long-distance. Blake was in Maryland, studying physics at Johns Hopkins. Karen was in California, studying UX and web development at UC Berkeley. Every month or so Blake would fly across the country to visit.

In June 2012, after Karen graduated with her master's degree and got her first programming job, Blake spent the summer with her. They ate a lot of burritos and sushi and hiked together in the beautiful Bay Area.

In June 2013, Blake decided to leave grad school and join Karen in California. He soon got a job programming embedded systems for a small startup in Mountain View. He and Karen moved together into an apartment in Silicon Valley.

On January 11, 2014, Karen and Blake hiked up Buena Vista Park in foggy San Francisco. Karen asked Blake if he wanted to get married. Blake said yes.

What will your names be after the wedding?

We wanted our new family to have a common name, but we did not want to snub either of our family names. Therefore, we are taking a new married surname, Tölva. After the wedding, we will go by Cortland Michael Setlow Tölva and Karen Arla Rustad Tölva.

How do I type the ö in Tölva?

On a Mac, type Option-u, then the letter o. On Windows, type Ctrl-Shift-:, then the letter o.
